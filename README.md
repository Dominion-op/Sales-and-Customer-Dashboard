## Dashboards Included  

### 1. Sales Dashboard  
A performance-focused dashboard designed to analyze overall company sales and profitability.  

**Key Features:**  
- Displays key metrics: **Total Sales**, **Profit**, and **Quantity Sold**  
- **Monthly Sales Trends** and **Year-over-Year comparison**  
- Highlights **Top Categories** and **Top Products**  
- Includes **interactive filters** for region, category, and date range  
- Utilizes **KPI cards** for quick insights  

---

### 2. Customer Dashboard  
A customer-centric dashboard that tracks performance, retention, and profitability metrics.  

**Key Features:**  
- **Customer segmentation** by purchase frequency and spending  
- **Profitability analysis** by customer  
- **Geographical insights** showing customer distribution  
- **Top 10 customers** based on revenue and profit  
- **Interactive filters** for deeper insights
